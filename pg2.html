Instructions

Please note that these self-assessment quizzes are not diagnostic tools. They are intended to provide insight into your mental health. Based on your results, we will guide you to resources for additional help.

  • Read each question carefully and answer honestly.

  • If your results suggest a need for support, links to resources and professional help will be provided.
